*BJT CIRCUIT
*Above is the title of this program, and now begin to discribe the circuit:
*VCC 1 0 12V
*VIN 5 0 SIN(0 0.3 100KHz)
*R1  1 2 110K
*R2  2 0 11K
*RC  1 3 30K
*RE  4 0 2.0K
*C1  5 2 0.1U
*Q1  3 2 4 QNPN
*Discription finished. Now set the BJT's parameters:
*.MODEL  QNPN NPN
*These are operating codes:
*.OP
*.TRAN 0.5us 20us
*.PROBE
*.plot tran v(2) v(3)
*And the program ending code.
*.option post
